# GoAi Technologies HRMS - User Guide

Welcome to the **GoAi Technologies Human Resource Management System (HRMS)**. This portal is designed to streamline our internal operations, from onboarding new talent to daily task management and attendance.

---

## 1. Dashboard Overview
The Dashboard is your command center. It provides:
- **Attendance Status**: Real-time "Clock In" and "Clock Out" functionality.
- **Leave Summary**: Quick access to request leaves and view your remaining balance.
- **Quick Stats (Admins)**: Real-time count of active employees, presence, and team members on leave.

## 2. Employee Directory
Manage and view the organization's workforce details in one place.
- **Profile Cards**: View employee photos, contact details, and designations.
- **Search & Filter**: Quickly find colleagues by name, email, or department.
- **Admin Controls**:
  - **Account Management**: Admins can securely send password reset links to staff.
  - **Role Assignment**: Assign roles such as Super Admin, HR Admin, or Team Manager to control system access.

## 3. Attendance & Time Tracking
Stay on top of your schedule with the automated attendance module.
- **Digital Muster**: Automated logging of check-in and check-out times.
- **History**: View your historical attendance records.
- **HR Oversite**: HR managers can review organization-wide attendance reports for payroll and compliance.

## 4. Leave Management
GoAi's paperless leave request system.
- **Request Flow**: Submit requests for Annual, Sick, or Casual leave with supporting reasons.
- **Approval Workflow**: Managers receive notifications and can approve or reject requests with comments.
- **Tracking**: Monitor the status of your applications (Pending, Approved, or Rejected) in real-time.

## 5. Task Management (Muster Board)
Our internal project management tool for daily work tracking.
- **Kanban Board**: Visualize tasks in columns (Pending, Ongoing, Completed).
- **Priority Tracking**: Focus on what matters with High, Medium, and Low priority tags.
- **Daily Work Logs**: Record the hours spent and a brief summary of daily accomplishments to keep the team aligned.

## 6. Recruitment & Application Tracking
The recruitment pipeline manages candidate's journey from application to hire.
- **Pipeline Stages**: Track applicants through stages like *Reviewing*, *Shortlisted*, and *Hired*.
- **Resume Portal**: Secure access to candidate resumes and skill profiles.
- **Conversion**: Seamlessly transition successful candidates from the recruitment tracker into the onboarding flow.

## 7. Digital Onboarding Wizard
A 4-step automated process for new GoAi interns and employees.
1. **Bio-Data**: Securely confirm residential and personal details.
2. **Document Vault**: Upload identity proofs and academic certificates for verification.
3. **Offer Letter**: View and digitally accept your official internship/employment offer.
4. **NDA & Security**: Digitally sign the Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) to complete your setup and receive portal access.
